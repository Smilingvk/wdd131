About Royal Chess Club

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Royal Chess Club has been the premier destination for chess enthusiasts in our community. What started as a small group of passionate players meeting in a local library has grown into a thriving organization with over 250 active members ranging from beginners to grandmasters.

Our mission is to promote the ancient game of chess as both an intellectual pursuit and a means of building community connections. We believe chess teaches valuable life skills including strategic thinking, patience, and graceful handling of both victory and defeat.
