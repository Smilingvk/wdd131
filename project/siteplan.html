Royal Chess Club

Site Name & Logo

Royal Chess Club – A distinguished name reflecting our commitment to excellence in chess. Logo will feature a stylized king’s crown and chess piece silhouette.

Site Purpose

The website will provide:

  • Information on club mission, history, and instructors
  • Interactive features: chess board, statistics, tournament brackets
  • Membership application and management
  • Resources: articles, tutorials, event calendar

User Scenarios

  1. As a beginner, where can I sign up for a beginner chess class?
  2. As a tournament player, how do I view upcoming tournament brackets?
  3. As a parent, how can I enroll my child in the youth program?

Color Schema

  • --primary-color: #2c1810 – Headings, primary buttons
  • --secondary-color: #8b4513 – Section headings, borders
  • --accent-color: #daa520 – CTAs, highlights
  • --light-bg: #f5f5dc – Section backgrounds
  • --white: #ffffff – Page background

Typography

  • Playfair Display – Used for all headings (H1, H2, H3)
  • Open Sans – Used for body text, navigation, buttons
